Recommended Equipment

We often get questions about which cooking utensils are best to make the best pancakes, so we decided to compile a list of our favourite kitchen tools! You can still make amazing pancakes without the specific items we mention, but it's just here as a resource if you ever want to replace or upgrade what you already own!

Non Stick Pan

No matter which brand, a nonstick pan is essential for making the perfect pancake. We love the Samuel Groves tri-ply stainless steel ceramic frying pan (available here), but if you're looking for an afforadable option, Ikea makes an amazing non-stick pan, which performs much better than its price might suggest. Buy it here.

Silicone Spatula

A silicone spatula is a must for easy fliping of your pancakes! Any silicone spatula will work, but we've tried and tested the OXO Good Grips Silicone Flexible Pancake Turner, and it's easily available here on Amazon! Simple!

Mixing Bowls

We're all guilty of having too many of one thing or another in our kitchens, and I'm afraid it's mixing bowls for me! I like to buy pretty ones that are both functional and decorative. Why not treat yourself to a Mason Cash bowl (available here) and pretend you're a contestant on the Great British Bake Off?
